Description

In this course, we will emphasize the use of computation to gain insight about quantitative problems with real data from the social sciences (for example, the smart community in Fuzhou). The course will cover computational thinking and quantitative reasoning, to better prepare the students for further coursework in data science, computer science, and statistics.

We aim to dive deeper into Python and its applications in data science in this course. Moreover, we will cover best practices with software engineering for data science and project management.
